# Use Case: International Trade via Apex Global Network

### The Scenario
A manufacturer in Lagos, Nigeria, is shipping 500 units of handmade goods to a boutique in London, UK.

### The Problem
Traditional shipping involves 5+ intermediaries, slow bank transfers, and paper documents that can be lost or forged.

### The Apex Solution (The Evidence)
1. **Initiation**: The manufacturer creates a shipment on the Apex Ledger. The goods are assigned a unique "Apex ID" (NFT).
2. **Global Transit**: As the crate moves from Lagos Port to London Gateway, each handler (Carrier, Customs, Port Authority) signs the Apex ID digitally.
3. **Automated Trust**: The buyer in London sees the immutable location in real-time.
4. **Final Settlement**: Upon arrival and buyer confirmation, the smart contract automatically settles the shipping fees in APEX tokens, bypassing international banking delays.

### Result
Trust is established through code, not promises. The transaction is faster, cheaper, and 100% transparent.
