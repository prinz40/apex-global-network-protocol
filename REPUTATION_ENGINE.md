# Apex Global Network: Trust & Reputation Engine

### The Apex Trust Score (ATS)
To ensure the integrity of the international logistics chain, every network participant (Carrier, Port, Customs Agent) is assigned a dynamic ATS.

### How it Works:
1. **Successful Delivery**: Completing a delivery on time and with an un-tampered "Apex ID" increases the agent's ATS.
2. **Dispute Resolution**: If a buyer reports a "Tampered" status and the blockchain evidence supports the buyer, the responsible carrier’s ATS is slashed.
3. **Staking for Trust**: High-level logistics partners must stake (lock) a portion of APEX tokens to act as "Master Carriers." If they act dishonestly, their stake is used to reimburse the victim.

### Impact:
This creates a self-policing global network where honesty is mathematically more profitable than fraud.
