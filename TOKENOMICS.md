# Apex Global Network: Tokenomics & Sustainability

### 1. The Utility Model
The APEX token is a non-inflationary utility asset. Its value is derived from its use as "gas" for the logistics tracking protocol and for settling cross-border service fees.

### 2. Supply Distribution
* **Ecosystem & Rewards (50%)**: Incentives for verified carriers and logistics partners.
* **Public Utility Pool (20%)**: Reserved for international trade participants.
* **Development Fund (15%)**: For long-term technical research and audits.
* **Founder & Team (15%)**: Locked to ensure long-term alignment.

### 3. The Founder's Lock (Proof of Commitment)
To ensure global trust, the Founder's allocation is subject to:
* **12-Month Cliff**: Zero tokens released for the first year.
* **48-Month Linear Vesting**: Gradual release over 4 years following the cliff.

### 4. Deflationary Mechanism
A percentage of every transaction fee on the Apex Network is "burned" (permanently removed from supply), ensuring that as the "Global Empire" grows, the token remains scarce and sustainable.
